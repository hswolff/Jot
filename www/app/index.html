Jot

The fastest way to write on your iPhone.

Seriously.

Tweet

Always Ready To Go

  • Never more than a screen away from typing
  • Add a new Jot with a swipe and a tap
  • Opens fast so you don't miss a thought!

Fast Gesture Driven Interface

  • Swipe right to see your list of Jots
  • Swipe left to act on your Jot
  • Swipe down down to hide the keyboard

Act On Your Jots

  • Share your Jot through E-mail, SMS, Clipboard, Twitter, and Facebook
  • Find your Jot's word and character count
  • More actions coming soon!
